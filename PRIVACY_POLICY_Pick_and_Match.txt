PRIVACY POLICY - PICK AND MATCH

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

HEIDI HEALTH TRADING PTY LTD ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application "Pick and Match" (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide
We may collect information that you provide directly to us when using the App, including:

- Any other information you choose to provide

1.2 Automatically Collected Information
When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)

We use the information we collect to:

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored locally on your device and may be backed up to cloud services provided by your device manufacturer (e.g., Google Play Services).

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
